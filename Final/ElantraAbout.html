Background

I first got started liking cars about 2 years ago. Starting to modify them was very recent. I first started by joining a group on facebook that has a group of owners that all own the same car with the same model. The group is called Elantra Sport Owners Group North America There is currently an upward number of 2,000 members that have the car. This group is what got me started on my mods. I was able to find sources to different car part manufactures for our cars. I got to speak with the companies social media promoters and got the information for the mods I will do and have done. The one that I wanted the most was my tune listed under My Mods page.
